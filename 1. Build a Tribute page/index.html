Dr. Gregory House

Dr. House is a fascinating and daringly cantankerous enigma.

About

Dr. Gregory House, M.D. is the title character of the American medical drama series House. Created by David Shore and portrayed by English actor Hugh Laurie, he leads a team of diagnosticians as the Head of Diagnostic Medicine at the fictional Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital in Princeton, New Jersey.

Personality

House frequently shows off his cunning and biting wit, and enjoys picking people apart and mocking their weaknesses, House accurately deduces people's motives and histories from aspects of their personality, appearance, and behavior His friend and colleague Wilson says that while some doctors have the "Messiah complex" (they need to "save the world"), House has the "Rubik's complex" (he needs to "solve the puzzle").
